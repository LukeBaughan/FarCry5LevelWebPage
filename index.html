Seized Settlement

Far Cry 5, Level Design

Gameplay

I was tasked with making a level in a level editor of my choice, so I decided to use Far Cry 5’s Arcade Editor to create a scenario whereby the player must navigate their way out of a enemy compound. They can either do this stealthy (by using suppressed weapons, melee attacks, sneaking past enemies) or loudly (using an arsenal of powerful weapons and explosives). The level takes players through a mixture of close quarters and open areas while still providing the player with the option of using short or long-range weaponry.

Feedback

I published Seized Settlements meaning that any Far Cry 5 player can play the level. In the first 5 days, it had 923 plays and received a positive rating overall by the community.
